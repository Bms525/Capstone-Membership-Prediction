Project Summary

Built and evaluated multiple predictive models (Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost) to identify likely responders. Model interpretability via SHAP highlighted key drivers such as purchase recency, household composition, and spending on premium categories (wine, meat, gold products). Findings inform targeted marketing to improve conversion and ROI.

Technologies: Python, pandas, scikit-learn, XGBoost, SHAP, matplotlib, Jupyter
